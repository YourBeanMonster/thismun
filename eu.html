European Union

Clothes torn, face streaked with dust, eyes wide with a mixture of fear and exhaustion— this is a young child refugee, clinging to the few belongings she has left, standing at the edge of a crowded border crossing. She is just one of millions displaced by conflict, yet her story speaks volumes about the urgent need for action. In the European Union Committee, you’ll have the chance to create a comprehensive protocol that could offer relief to children like her and countless others. The time to act is now—join us and take on the responsibility of crafting solutions for those who have been forced to leave everything behind.
