Conference Information

Tsinghua International School Model United Nations (THISMUN V) is a three-day simulation where high school students take on the roles of world leaders to debate and draft solutions to global issues. With a focus on democracy and collaboration, THISMUN V provides a safe and inspiring space for delegates to develop skills in public speaking, negotiation, and teamwork.

· Date: March 21st - 23rd
· Venue: Campus of Tsinghua International School
· Opens to: Secondary students from G8 to G12
· Primary Language of Instruction: English
