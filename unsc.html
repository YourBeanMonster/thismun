United Nations Security Council

Over 500,000 lives have been lost, and more than 12 million people have been displaced—yet the Syrian Civil War rages on, leaving deep scars on the region and the world. Join the Security Council at this year’s Model United Nations conference and step into the shoes of international leaders tasked with confronting this crisis. As a delegate, you’ll have the opportunity to engage in high-stakes discussions to mitigate the war’s catastrophic impact, drive humanitarian aid, and forge a path toward lasting peace.
